Hi,

I also noticed some problems when performing transfers in mixed  
environments lately. I first thought the issue was due to some  
unorthodox use of meetme rooms in connection with local channels, but  
then it also occured with a transfer _to_ a Zap channel.

My problem is characterized by the following phenomenon:

1. You attempt a transfer and it doesn't work. Possibly the phone  
won't react properly.
2. If you type "show channels" at the asterisk prompt, it will give  
you an infinite list of the same channel all over again.
3. You need to restart asterisk.

The problem seems to occur only with 1.4.

By the way it's pretty nasty that you can really mess up the global  
channel list of asterisk in such a way.

We shall get working on the problem shorty, but it would be very  
helpful if any of you could reproduce it  and / or get his/her hands  
on it.


Kind Regards,
-David.
