Hi,

I have a working chan_sccp with Asterisk 1.4.0. I have posted my patch
is posted at https://developer.berlios.de/patch/index.php.  It is
against the svn (which is why it is such a small patch :). I only have a
limited setup to test against, so please beware, but so far so good :)

(gtalk etc).., so 1.4.0 is a natural progression and have the ability to
break this one :)
If it was prod I would probably hold off. 

Oh, and I went to chan_skinny initially with 1.4.0, but didn't want to
spend time converting the configs. (too lazy?)
