On 1/11/07, Carsten Mueller <mueller at intertrend.de> wrote:
>
> And which are the other ways?
> How can I set a ringtone for a configured line on a 7970?


Ok, here is an example:

exten => s,1,Dial(SCCP/101/aa1w)

This will cause the phone to autoanswer, but only the caller can talk (1
way).  Change aa1w to aa2w and you can have a two way conversation.

You can also use ringer= instead, such as:

 exten => s,1,Dial(SCCP/101/ringer=inside)

WIll ring with the inside tone.  You can use inside, feature, silent, and
urgent.  It defaults to outside.  I just noticed this in the code, it
appears you can use 1w or 2w instead of aa1w and aa2w.  I might be reading
that wrong, though.
