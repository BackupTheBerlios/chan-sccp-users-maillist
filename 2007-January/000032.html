And, because text is accepted and passed on in speeddial, you could have the
speeddial set to dial bank and then include:

exten => bank,1,Dial(Zap/g1/5555555wwwww2)

or something similar.  I guess even put in SpeedDial01 as the name and then
use SpeedDial01 in your dial plan.
