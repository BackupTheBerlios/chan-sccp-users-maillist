Hi Steve,

basically, the answer is yes, but limited. Limited in that you cannot  
see the number of the call or whether it is ringing, connected or in  
whatever way active. But yes, it is possible and works -- just setup a  
hinted speeddial and define a hint for the corresponding zaptel  
channel. I'm not sure if this works without bristuff, however, so  
maybe you should just check out.
