we recognize a little bug. If you get a second telephone call and you pickup
the phone without choosing which call you would like to answer, the newest
one is taken. We think it would make sense that the oldest one is taken at
first. Of course you can toggle between both calls. But in the daily work
you forget to do so.

Maybe this can be fixed quite easily, if all agree to the strategy first in
first taken.
