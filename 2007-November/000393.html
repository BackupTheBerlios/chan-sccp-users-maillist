Hi,

an inevitable issue we're running into lately is related to fact that  
we now have two different mailing lists on the same topic.
In time we will most certainly start losing feedback if we only post  
to one list, on the other hand posts to both lists can be annoying or  
even distracting for those people who subscribed to both.

To make life easier for all of us, I'd suggest that everybody on the  
berlios list who is somehow to participate in development or  
deployment of the _new_ chan-sccp-b driver should join the sourceforge  
list and post messages regarding that project only to that list.

Please don't get me wrong: I do not seek to discourage use of the  
berlios list at all. However, people who recently learned about chan- 
sccp-b on Sourceforge should also have a chance to participate in the  
development discussions.

Kind regards,
-David.
