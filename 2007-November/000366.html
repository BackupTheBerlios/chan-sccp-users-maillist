Hi David,

> Can anyone recommend firmware versions for 7941/7961 (fairly hard to
> work out what the
> version number is from the filenames!). As you say, there seems to be
> little point in using
> the latest firmware unless you are actually working with Call Manager.

We have a couple of 7941's and use 7941-7961 SCCP 7.0.3:

cnu41.2-7-6-26.sbn
CVM41.2-0-2-26.sbn
Jar41.2-9-2-26.sbn
TERM41.7-0-3-0S.loads
term41.default.loads
term61.default.loads

For all phones we use:

<dateTemplate>D.M.Y</dateTemplate>
<timeZone>W. Europe Standard/Daylight Time</timeZone>

The 7940's / 60's are fine for the 7941/70's they need this in
chan_sccp.conf:

tzoffset    = -1

To show the correct time :-)

> Timezones - My server's clock is on UTC/GMT and correctly changes the
> local time for DST.
> As you say, I thought 7940/7960s just take the local time via sccp and
> also take the date
